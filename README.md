# AI Disaster Rescue System 🚨  
### Offline-First Emergency Communication & Rescue Coordination Platform

AI Disaster Rescue System is a next-generation disaster response application designed to operate in **zero-internet and low-connectivity zones** during emergencies such as floods, earthquakes, landslides, and wildfires.

The platform enables **victims, civilians, and rescue teams** to communicate, share SOS alerts, and coordinate rescue operations using **peer-to-peer mesh networking, offline communication protocols, and intelligent prioritization systems**.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

During natural disasters, traditional communication infrastructure often fails:

- Mobile towers collapse
- Internet becomes unavailable
- Victims cannot request help
- Rescue teams lack real-time location data
- Response becomes slow and inefficient

This leads to preventable delays and loss of life.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

AI Disaster Rescue System bridges the gap between disaster occurrence and rescue execution by providing:

✅ Offline emergency communication  
✅ SOS broadcasting in no-network zones  
✅ Peer-to-peer mesh connectivity  
✅ Victim location sharing  
✅ Rescue team dashboard  
✅ AI-powered alert prioritization  
✅ Sync to cloud when connectivity returns  

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📡 Offline Mesh Communication
Devices connect directly using:

- Bluetooth
- WiFi Direct
- Local peer discovery

Messages are relayed from device to device without internet.

---

### 🆘 Emergency SOS Alerts

Victims can instantly send:

- Distress messages
- GPS location
- Emergency category
- Medical status (optional)

---

### 📍 Live Location Sharing

Share current or last-known location for faster rescue operations.

---

### 🤖 AI Prioritization Engine

The system intelligently prioritizes rescue requests based on:

- Severity level
- Number of people affected
- Time elapsed
- Medical urgency
- Repeated distress signals

---

### 🚑 Rescue Team Dashboard

Dedicated interface for responders to view:

- Nearby SOS alerts
- Priority victims
- Clustered emergency zones
- Route suggestions

---

### ☁️ Delayed Cloud Sync

Once internet connectivity returns:

- All offline messages sync automatically
- Reports uploaded to central system
- Data available for authorities

---

```text
Victim Device

   ↓
